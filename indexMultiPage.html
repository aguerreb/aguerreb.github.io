Cecilia Aguerrebere

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Prof. Guillermo Sapiro's Lab at the ECE (Electrical and Computer Engineering Department) at Duke University.

I obtained my Phd in Signal and Image processing from Télécom ParisTech, France and in Electrical Engineering from Universidad de la República, Uruguay (joint Phd program) in 2014.

I did my Bs. and M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Universidad de la República, Uruguay and a M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics (MVA) at École Normale Supérieure de Cachan, France.
